Starmine Handmade

In March 2013, I began Starmine Handmade with several friends at a small artist's table at a local anime convention. Starmine Handmade is a store that sells cute plush toys, jewellery, and art prints. Since its inception, Starmine Handmade has made appearances all over Texas at other conventions including Houston’s Anime Matsuri, Galveston’s Oni-con, San Antonio’s San Japan, and Dallas’s A-kon.

Webstore

Starmine's webstore is built within Storenvy. I made all of the graphical elements, took pictures of my merchandise, and keep the inventory up to date.
