Space Invaders: Pokemon Edition

My teammate, Xingtong Huang and I worked on a version of a popular game "Space Invaders" for our final project in EE319K "Introduction to Embedded Systems."

Demonstration

Sofware

The game was coded in ARM assembly and C language and run on a EK-TM4C123GXL microcontroller. We used Keil uVision as our main software development system.

Hardware

Circuit diagrams were planned out using PCBArtist.
