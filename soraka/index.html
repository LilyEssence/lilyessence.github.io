Costume Fabrication: Dryad Soraka

Started in the winter of 2012, Soraka was completed in March 2013. Soraka is a fictional champion in the popular multiplayer online battle arena, "League of Legends." Her costume consisted of a dress, vest, hooves, fur leg warmers, a staff, wig, horn, and prosthetic ears. She was revamped several times and worn to events over the next two years before being gently retired.

San Japan 2014

Soraka was worn on the Saturday of a annual anime convention called San Japan held in San Antonio, Texas.

Reference

References for Soraka were gathered from in game 3D models as well as official illustrated cover art. In the game, Soraka has several visual 'skins' or different models that alter her appearance. I decided to focus on her Dryad skin with it's more natural skin color and green accents.
